Urban Renovation

Strategic infill development designed to harmonize with and elevate the existing urban fabric through vision-led execution.

Earl’s Court

Earl’s Court is located on 1.3 acres at the entrance to the Old Village of Mt. Pleasant, SC. Its 27 homes range in size from 1,100 to 2,400 square feet. In addition, a renovated historic building houses two art galleries. And coming soon, a 23-room boutique inn and restaurant!

Morris Square

Developed in the early 2000s on a vacant one-acre lot in the Radcliffborough neighborhood of downtown Charleston, Morris Square consisted of 5,000 square feet of commercial space, 32 condominium flats and attached and detached single-family homes, and two Charleston city parks. All on one acre!

Catfiddle Street

Comprised of 24 residential homes on just under an acre of land, Catfiddle Street represents a 21st-century evolution of organic infill growth. This project utilizes a time-honored pattern of development to create an intimate, walkable micro-neighborhood in the heart of the old city.
